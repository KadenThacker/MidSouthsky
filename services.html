Our Services

Mid-South Sky offers comprehensive drone photography and videography services for a variety of industries and needs.

Aerial Photography & Videography Services

  • Real Estate Photography - High-quality aerial shots of residential and commercial properties
  • Construction Site Documentation - Progress monitoring and site surveys
  • Event Coverage - Weddings, festivals, concerts, and corporate events
  • Marketing & Advertising - Dynamic footage for commercials and promotions
  • Property Inspections - Roof, gutter, and structural inspections
  • Agricultural Surveying - Crop monitoring and land analysis
  • 3D Mapping & Modeling - Topographic maps and 3D property models
  • Insurance Documentation - Damage assessment and claim support
  • Live Event Streaming - Real-time aerial footage for broadcasts
  • Search & Rescue Support - Aerial search operations
